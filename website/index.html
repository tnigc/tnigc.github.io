About Us

Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII) goes way back to 2001 when it was established by the Government of Tamil Nadu. EDII looks to create an innovative startup culture through a variety of events and initiatives.
 
Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC) by EDII is a series of events which kindles budding ideas to evolve into would-be startups. TNIGC serves as a platform for teams to interact with experienced community members and expert mentors while competing for a government grant. Three winning teams will be awarded seed money of 5 lakh each.

Event Schedule

Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge
Sprint
Sprint

Description Rules


  1. Team of atleast two can register for the Sprint round.
  2. The prerequisite here is that the team must have a prototype of their project.
  3. Contestants will work on their ideas for 48 hours.
  4. Apart from the contest, in a unique experiment, teams are topped up with virtual money to invest in other teams
  5. Chennai, Madurai and Coimbatore are the cities where Sprint is going to be held. Exact Venues and Dates will be announced later.
  6. The difference between a sprint and a typical hackathon is that Sprint expects a working prototype ready at the get-go.We will provide the teams with the community people who will help in enhancing the prototype idea and to develop the prototype.
  7. There will be a Jury Panel which will scrutinize the idea & prototype of each team and will finalise teams for Bootcamp


  1. The event is open to all categories. Ideas from any domain can register.
  2. Hackathon will be conducted at three locations - Chennai, Coimbatore, Thanjavur.
  3. All the participating teams need to provide a viable prototype or Proof of Concept.
  4. Teams should have testimonials from at least two individuals from startup community/ Certificate of merit from any two recognised startup events.
  5. If the registered team is an incorporated startup, the date of registration should not exceed one year on 1/12/2018.
  6. Only teams which are selected after the pre-scrutiny can participate in the event after payment of Rs. 2000/- (per team) through our online portal.
  7. The decision of the jury is final.
  8. Teams those tops the list in hackathon will be attending Bootcamp in Chennai.
BootCamp
Bootcamp


  1. The top teams from Sprint will be chosen to attend the 3-day boot camp where things shift into high gear.
  2. The Bootcamp promises to be a constructive session of dynamic approaches. With the help of mentor expertise, teams will have the chance to crystallize their ideas and bring out the best in themselves.
PitchFest
PitchFest
Description Rules


  1. The teams which attend the Bootcamp will go on to pitch before a panel of renowned investors and government officials.
  2. The pitchfest is the final leg of the event where participants make their case before a jury consisting of venture capitalists and angel investors.
  3. The top 3 teams will be awarded a government grant of 5 lakh each to take their startups forward with continued support from EDII.


  1. A maximum of 4 minutes will be allotted for every team to pitch their ideas.
  2. The Q&A session will go on for a time span of 5-10 minutes.
  3. The teams will be judged based on a fixed criterion.
  4. The team needs to be present for the complete session.
  5. Certificates and prizes will be provided based on the creativity and execution of the team’s pitch.
  6. The decision of the jury is final.

Got Questions?

Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge
What is TNIGC?
Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC) is an initiative by Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII) to support ideas of budding startups and enhance the future of tomorrow. The Government of Tamil Nadu established it in 2001.
Who can attend?
Do you think out of the box? TNIGC is where to make your dreams come true! We are looking for teams with creative ideas and thrive to make the world a better place. Bring great minds together and form a group of not more than three. We’re looking for teams with a prototype. You should complete 21 years of age to board the knowledge train...
How can I apply?
Fill the registration form through the online portal. You will get a payment link after a pre-screening process. The process will be completed after you pay Rs. 2000 through the link. Consider the confirmation mail as the entry ticket.
What should I bring?
To start with your laptops and any special kit that could be imperative to your project. You’re also welcome to bring additional technology to support your working prototype if necessary. If you have specific requirements, please reach out us prior to your presentation. We want to make it perfect for you. Don’t worry if you forget something. We’re always here to help.
Where do we sign up for the event?
It’s already begun! What are you waiting for? You can sign up below by January 26th, 2019 latest by 11:59:59 PM. No registration fee is required. Hurry up! 343 seats to go, Clock is ticking.
How much does it cost?
Applying is free. However, teams that are selected in the pre-screening will have to pay a fee of Rs.2000/- to continue to Sprint.
What is the goal of this competition?
It seems you’ve reached the FAQ a bit early ☺ Anyway, here’s a heads up.
  1. You got a great idea.
  2. You want to publicize it and earn recognition.
  3. Plus you’ll be given a chance to implement your idea.
How cool is that!
How many participants?
Your team must consist of three or less. Please don’t exceed. We’re looking more for quality, not quantity.
What is the prize amount?
Three winning teams will be awarded seed money of 5 lakh each.
What should my project be about?
Our world has no shortage of real problems to solve that affect our everyday lives and those of future generations. These large-scale issues span across cities, industries, time zones and cultures. We believe technology can play a critical role in building a better future by bringing smart people together to solve them. Your project can be anything which helps address these problems. Let’s see what’s in the horizon.
What if I don’t want to present my work?
You might think your project isn’t impressive enough or is incomplete. Presenting your hack gives you a chance to be proud of what you’ve done, and working for Social Good is something to be proud of. You may not want to present it, but you should present it anyway!
I want to volunteer. How can I help?
We’d love your help! Please fill out this form and we’ll get in touch with you to discuss where you’d fit in.
What about food?
Maslow came up with a hierarchy of needs. However, we know that these rank amongst the most basic, because Maslow has never been to a TNIGC, let alone organizing one! In short, we’ve got it covered. (Lunch & Snacks)
