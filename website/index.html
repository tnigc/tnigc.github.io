About Us

Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII) goes way back to 2001 when it was established by the Government of Tamil Nadu. EDII looks to create an innovative startup culture through a variety of events and initiatives.
 
Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC) by EDII is a series of events which kindles budding ideas to evolve into would-be startups. TNIGC serves as a platform for teams to interact with experienced community members and expert mentors while competing for a government grant. Three winning teams will be awarded seed money of 5 lakh each.

Sprint:

  1. The event is open to all categories. Ideas from any domain can register.
  2. Hackathon will be conducted at three locations - Chennai, Coimbatore, Thanjavur.
  3. All the participating teams need to provide a viable prototype or Proof of Concept.
  4. Teams should have testimonials from at least two individuals from startup community/ Certificate of merit from any two recognised startup events.
  5. If the registered team is an incorporated startup, the date of registration should not exceed one year on 1/12/2018.
  6. Only teams which are selected after the pre-scrutiny can participate in the event after payment of Rs. 2500/- (per team) through our online portal.
  7. The decision of the jury is final.
  8. Teams those tops the list in hackathon will be attending Bootcamp in Chennai.

Pitch Fest:

  1. A maximum of 4 minutes will be allotted for every team to pitch their ideas.
  2. The Q&A session will go on for a time span of 5-10 minutes.
  3. The teams will be judged based on a fixed criterion.
  4. The team needs to be present for the complete session.
  5. Certificates and prizes will be provided based on the creativity and execution of the team’s pitch.
  6. The decision of the jury is final.


 

Benefits:

  1. Community network
  2. TNIGC provide a platform to enhance the team through community support.
  3. A total grant worth of Rs.15 Lakhs for the winning startups from Tamil Nadu Government.
  4. Incubator access.
  5. Mentor support.

Event Schedule

Sprint

Read More
Sprint
  1. Team of atleast two can register for the Sprint round.
  2. The prerequisite here is that the team must have a prototype of their project.
  3. Contestants will work on their ideas for 48 hours.
  4. Apart from the contest, in a unique experiment, teams are topped up with virtual money to invest in other teams
  5. Chennai, Madurai and Coimbatore are the cities where Sprint is going to be held. Exact Venues and Dates will be announced later.
  6. The difference between a sprint and a typical hackathon is that Sprint expects a working prototype ready at the get-go.We will provide the teams with the community people who will help in enhancing the prototype idea and to develop the prototype.
  7. There will be a Jury Panel which will scrutinize the idea & prototype of each team and will finalise teams for Bootcamp

Bootcamp

Read More
Bootcamp
  1. The top teams from Sprint will be chosen to attend the 3-day boot camp where things shift into high gear.
  2. The Bootcamp promises to be a constructive session of dynamic approaches. With the help of mentor expertise, teams will have the chance to crystallize their ideas and bring out the best in themselves.

PitchFest

Read More
PitchFest
  1. The teams which attend the Bootcamp will go on to pitch before a panel of renowned investors and government officials.
  2. The pitchfest is the final leg of the event where participants make their case before a jury consisting of venture capitalists and angel investors.
  3. The top 3 teams will be awarded a government grant of 5 lakh each to take their startups forward with continued support from EDII.
