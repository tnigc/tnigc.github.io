About

Do you think out of the box? Do you dream about creating a better tomorrow through innovation? Are you itching to startup? The Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC) is for you.

The Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC), an initiative by Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII), aims to promote innovation and startup culture by providing financial grants to budding entrepreneurs. The event comprises a bootcamp - to educate entrepreneurs - and a pitchfest session - to decide the winners of the grant. Five winning teams from the program will be awarded with seed money of ₹5 lakh each to support their venture.

How Does It Work?

Sprint

Description
  1. Team of atleast two can register for the Sprint round.
  2. The prerequisite here is that the team must have a prototype of their project.
  3. Contestants will work on their ideas for 48 hours.
  4. Apart from the contest, in a unique experiment, teams are topped up with virtual money to invest in other teams
  5. The venue for sprint will be announced shortly. Exact Venues and Dates will be announced later.
  6. The difference between a sprint and a typical hackathon is that Sprint expects a working prototype ready at the get-go.We will provide the teams with the community people who will help in enhancing the prototype idea and to develop the prototype.
  7. There will be a Jury Panel which will scrutinize the idea & prototype of each team and will finalise teams for Bootcamp
  8. Exact venue will be announced shortly.
  9. Sprint has various sessions including.
  10.        Design sprint to enhance and validate the idea
  11.        Growth time with mentors to help in fine-tuning ideas.
  12.        Prototype sprint to improve the quality of the prototype through feedback mechanisms
  13.        Final Pitching to select well-deserved teams for the Bootcamp

Rules
  1. The event is open to all categories. Ideas from any domain can register.
  2. Hackathon will be held at Chennai.
  3. The team should have a working prototype of their idea or Proof of concept.
  4. If an incorporated startup wishes to participate, the date of registration should not be more than a year as of 1/12/2018.
  5. Depending upon the predetermined criteria teams are selected after the pre-screening process.
  6. To continue forward the team has to make a payment of ₹2000/- through our online portal.
  7. The seat of the teams in sprint will be confirmed on first paid first served basis.
  8. Teams those tops the list in hackathon will be attending Bootcamp.

Bootcamp
The secret of getting ahead is getting started. Become the super team which can achieve the results they seek.

Step 2

Pitchfest March 22, 2020

Chennai

Know more
PitchFest
  1. The PitchFest is the final leg of the event where the teams shall present their startup idea before an esteemed jury of investors and entrepreneurs.
  2. The top 5 teams will be awarded a grant of INR 5 Lakhs each to take their startups forward with continued support from EDII.

Rules
  1. A maximum of 4 minutes will be allotted for every team to pitch their ideas.
  2. The Q&A session shall last a span of 5-10 minutes.
  3. The teams will be judged based on a fixed criterion.
  4. Teams needs to be present for the complete session.
  5. Certificates and prizes will be provided based on the creativity and execution of the team’s pitch.
  6. The decision of the jury is final.

Got Questions?

What is TNIGC?

The Tamil Nadu Innovation Grand Challenge (TNIGC), an initiative by Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII), aims to promote innovation and startup culture by providing financial grants to budding entrepreneurs. The event comprises a bootcamp - to educate entrepreneurs - and a pitchfest session - to decide the winners of the grant. Five winning teams from the program will be awarded with seed money of ₹5 lakh each to support their venture.

Who can attend?

Indian citizens who have achieved 21 years of age and are raring to startup can apply for the program. The program looks to identify promising entrepreneurs with a prototype or proof of concept.

How can I apply?

After filling this registration form, you will get a payment link after a pre-screening process. The process will be completed after you pay ₹2000 through the link. Consider the confirmation mail as the entry ticket.

What should I bring?

To start with, your laptops and any special kits that could be imperative to your project. You’re also welcome to bring additional technology to support your working prototype if necessary. If you have specific requirements, please reach out to us prior to your presentation. We want to make it perfect event for you. Don’t worry if you forget something. We’re always here to help.

Where do we sign up for the event?

It’s already begun! What are you waiting for? It’s already begun! What are you waiting for? You can sign up below by March 17, 2020 latest by 11.59.59. No registration fee is required.

How much does it cost?

Applying is free. However, teams that are selected in the pre-screening will have to pay a fee of ₹2000/- to participate in the event.
